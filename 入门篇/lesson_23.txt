1. this is the arrival lounge.
a. arrival n. 到达
the fans are waiting for the arrival of the movie star.
arrive v. 动词
when will the plane arrive?
arrive 之后常接介词at 或者 in 再接地方名词 如下列用法
arrive at + 地方 (建筑物 如 邮局 车站等) 到达...
arrive in + 地方 (大地方如城市 国家等) 到达...
they will arrive at the hotel before six o'clockl
the plane will arrive in New York tonight.

b. arrival lounge 入境大厅
departure lounge 出境大厅
上列两个大厅均在机场的航站大厦 (air terminal)
里面 lounge 是大厅或者休息室的意思
如 teacher's lounge 教师休息室
但旅馆 饭店内的大厅都叫做 lobby ['lɑbi]

3. There! the check-in counters are over there.
a. check in counter['kaʊntɚ]
指办理报到收手续的柜台, 在机场办理的是登记手续, 在旅馆则是
办理住宿登记的手续
b. over there 在那里
over here 在这里
上列中的over作为强调用的副词. over there/here 为目视距离内
的指示用法, 通常亦可在其前加上另一个强调副词right, 形成right over there
和 right over here 此外 亦可将over省略, 形成 right there/here 但此时无距离的含义.

who is the man standing over there?
the thing we are looking for is right over here.
i will be right here waiting for you.
right 也可以用来强调介词短语, over不可
there's a beautiful bountain right in the middle of the park.
fountain 喷泉

4. don't we have to go pay the airport tax first?
= dont we have to go and pay the airport tax first?
我们不用先去付机场税么
a. 英文中两个动词在一个句子时, 必须有连接词链接, 否则为错误句型
但是 go 和 come 以原形动词出现时. 则可将其后连接词and 省略, 直接
加另一个动词.
they sing, dance happily day after day 错误句型
they sing and dance happily day after day. 正确用法

please go and buy me a newspaper.
= please go buy me a newspaper.
when you have time come and visit me.
= when you have time come visit me.

b. pay 为动词 表示"(付)钱"的意思, 下列为常见的用法:
pay + 钱 + for...　付钱买...
pay sb + 钱 + for .. 为...付钱给某人...
i will not pay one hundred dollars for the toy.
she pays me a lot of money for doing this work.

c. the airport tax 机场税
政府向人民征收机场建设费

first of all 代替 first of all 只能放置于句首.
